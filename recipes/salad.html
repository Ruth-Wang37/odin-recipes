Avocado & Egg salad

The image above is of a bowl of egg salad. The recipe is quite simple to make and takes little time.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 cherry tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of ½ lemon
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil eggs → peel → cut.
  2. Slice avocado.
  3. Slice tomato
  4. Arrange on greens.
  5. Drizzle olive oil + lemon juice.
  6. Garnich with cilantro(optional)
  7. Season and serve..
