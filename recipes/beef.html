Beef Wraps

The image above is of a beef wrap served on the wooden board. The recipe is quite simple to make and takes little time.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas or chapati
  • beef strips
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Sauce (mayo, yogurt, or chili)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Warm tortillas.
  2. Lay meat in center.
  3. Add veggies + sauce.
  4. Season lightly.
  5. Fold sides → roll tightly.
  6. Toast lightly on pan
